The Reality of Modular

Not a Trailer.
Not a Kit Home.
Your Dream Home.

These aren't manufactured homes. They're not trailers. They're fully custom, architect-designed homes that happen to be built with factory precision instead of on-site sticks.

The only thing that's different is how efficient the process is — and how much money ends up in your pocket instead of a contractor's. Same codes. Same finishes. Same permanent foundation. Identical resale value.

Every home in this gallery was built to MA state building codes, sits on a permanent foundation, and was delivered in a fraction of the time it would have taken stick-built. Your neighbors won't know — until you tell them.

The Moment Everything Changes

What Is
Set Day?

Set day is when the factory-built modules arrive on your property and a crane lifts them into place — section by section — onto the foundation — pile or concrete, depending on your site — that's been waiting. It typically takes one day. One single day, and your home has walls, a roof, and a shape.

Watching your home go up in a single day — while your friends are still waiting 8 months for their stick-built to frame up — is a different feeling entirely.
