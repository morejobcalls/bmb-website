Why BMB in Norwell

Large Lots.
Custom Design.
Built for Norwell.

Norwell is one of the South Shore's most distinctive communities — wooded, unhurried, with larger lots that give homeowners the space and privacy that's increasingly rare in eastern Massachusetts. For families who own land in Norwell and want to build a home that complements that character, BMB offers an approach that traditional contractors simply can't match on timeline or cost.

Norwell homeowners building new typically encounter stick-built quotes of $400–$600 per square foot and timelines stretching 15–18 months. BMB delivers the same custom home at approximately $250 per square foot in 8–12 weeks. For a 3,000 square foot home on a wooded Norwell lot, that's a potential savings of $450,000–$1,050,000 compared to traditional construction — money that stays with your family instead of going to construction overhead.

Mike Biviano understands Norwell's character and what homeowners here are looking for: quality craftsmanship, thoughtful design, and a process that doesn't turn your land into a 12-month construction zone. With factory-built modules, the on-site build phase is dramatically shorter — your land is disturbed for weeks, not over a year. The result is a home that belongs in Norwell's landscape, built the way Norwell families expect.
