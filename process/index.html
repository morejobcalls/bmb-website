How It Works

The 12-Week
Dream Home System

How Plymouth County families move into their custom home in 8–12 weeks — while Mike handles every detail from design to keys.

The Mechanism

The reason BMB can deliver in 8–12 weeks isn't a gimmick — it's an engineering decision. Your home's modules are precision-built in a climate-controlled factory while Mike simultaneously prepares your site: foundation poured, permits pulled, utilities roughed in, engineering signed off. Traditional builders do those steps one after another. We run them in parallel. That single structural difference is what turns a 12-month project into a 12-week move-in.
