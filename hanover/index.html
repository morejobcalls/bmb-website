Hanover sits at the center of Plymouth County's fastest-growing residential market. Families along the Route 3 corridor have long found that traditional stick-built construction quotes — frequently topping $1 million for a custom home — price them out of building on land they already own. BMB changes that equation by delivering the same custom home in 8–12 weeks for roughly 25% less than traditional construction.

Mike Biviano has served families throughout the Hanover area for decades.
