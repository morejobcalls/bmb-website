## Full 9-Step Roadmap (from /roadmap page)

**Step 1: Site Evaluation & Project Planning**
Confirming property is suitable for building or renovation by reviewing zoning, elevation, setbacks, and coastal/FEMA requirements.

**Step 2: Choose Biviano as Your Builder**
Meet with the team to discuss goals, budget, and timeline, leveraging 40+ years of South Shore experience.

**Step 3: Secure Financing**
Connect with lenders who understand construction, modular homes, renovations, and elevation projects — or work with your preferred bank.

**Step 4: Design Your Home or Project**
Collaborate on layouts, finishes, materials, and structural options for modular homes, additions, renovations, or decks.

**Step 5: Finalize Your Order**
Complete permits, engineering, surveys, and material selections before factory orders or renovation timelines begin.

**Step 6: Construction Begins**
Foundations, elevations, framing, and structural work commence on-site while modular units build simultaneously at the factory.

**Step 7: Delivery & Setting**
Factory modules arrive and are professionally positioned on the foundation — becomes weather-tight same day.

**Step 8: On-Site Finish Work**
Flooring, trim, cabinetry, siding, decks, railings, plumbing, and electrical work completes with attention to detail.

**Step 9: Final Walkthrough & Enjoy**
Complete walkthrough confirms satisfaction, adjustments handled immediately, warranties and certificates provided.
